Ingredients

For the Egusi soup:

  • Melon Seeds: 2 cups ground Egusi (blended with a little water or onion to form a thick paste).
  • Palm Oil: 1.5 cooking spoons.
  • Proteins: 500g Beef (cubed), 1 piece of Stockfish, 2 pieces of Dry Fish (cleaned/deboned).
  • The Pepper Base: 3 Red Bell Peppers (Tatashe), 2 Scotch Bonnets (Atarodo), and 1 large Onion—all blended roughly.
  • Seasoning: 2 tablespoons ground Crayfish, 1 tablespoon Fermented Locust Beans (Iru), 2 seasoning cubes, and salt to taste.
  • Vegetables: 2 cups chopped fresh Spinach or Ugu leaves.

For the Pounded Yam:

  • Yam: 1 medium tuber of Puna Yam (white yam).
  • Water: Enough for boiling.
